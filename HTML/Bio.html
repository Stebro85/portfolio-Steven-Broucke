About me


Hi, I’m Steven Broucke, a 40-year-old from Menen, Belgium. I currently work as an assistant planner, a role that has helped me develop strong organizational skills, an eye for detail, and a good sense of problem-solving. Over time, I’ve realized that what truly excites me is technology — the creativity, logic, and constant learning that come with building things digitally. That passion has inspired me to take a big step and retrain myself as a full stack developer. It’s a new adventure that challenges me in the best way possible. I’m currently diving into programming, learning about front-end and back-end development, and discovering how all the pieces fit together to bring ideas to life on the web. While sometimes have moments of doubt — like anyone starting something new — I’m driven by a strong desire to improve myself and grow both personally and professionally. I believe that with consistency, curiosity, and effort, I can build a solid foundation in tech and shape a new career that truly excites me. I’m eager to keep learning, experimenting, and connecting with others in the tech community. My goal is simple: to keep moving forward, to keep improving, and to turn my curiosity into real skills that make an impact.
